App Features

Awesome Features

Voice Recording for Translation

Users can record their voice and receive instant translations, helping improve pronunciation and speaking skills

Live Score for Speaking

After speaking, users get real-time scores based on their accuracy and fluency, enabling quick progress tracking and improvement.

History of Recording and Scoring

Users can review past recordings and scores to track progress and identify areas for improvement in their English learning journey

Fully Secured

The app ensures complete data privacy and security, protecting all user information, recordings, and progress with advanced encryption and secure storage.

Cloud Storage

Users can save their recordings and progress in the cloud, ensuring easy access across devices and safeguarding their data from loss.

Fully Responsive

The app is designed to work seamlessly on all devices and screen sizes, providing a smooth and consistent experience whether on mobile, tablet, or desktop.
